FAQs

Who is the competent authority to issue of Fire NOC to Industries?

The Director General, State Disaster Response and Fire Services, A.P. Vijayawada is competent to issue Fire NOC.

Who should apply for Fire No Objection Certificate?

Any person proposing to construct a building of more than 15 meters height for commercial/business purpose, 18 meters and above height for residential purpose, and buildings of public congregation like schools, cinema halls, function halls, religious places, which are more than 500 Sq. Meter in plot area or 6 meters and above in height should apply to the Director General, AP State Disaster Response & Fire Services, Vijayawada for a No Objection certificate(NOC) along with such Fire Precautionary fee prescribed as Rs.10/- per Sq.Mtr., built-up area including basement/parking floors.

Time period for disposal of application?

The Application shall be processed and disposed within 60 days from the date of submission

How to pay the Challan amount?

Please visit the site and make payment of Fire Precautionary Fee through online payment the bank will generate the fee. The same can be uploaded.

How to calculate the challan amount ?

(i) Fee for provisional NOC= Non Refundable Fire Precaution Fee @ Rs.10/- per Sq. Meter of total built up area in all floors including Stilts/Cellars/Basements.
(ii) Fee for Occupancy NOC:
 a) If obtained Provisional NOC = 10 % Statutory fee paid on built-up area during Provisional NOC.(5years fee have to pay at a time)
 b) If applied directly for Occupancy then [Non Refundable Fire Precaution Fee @ Rs.10/- per Sq. Meter of total built up area in all floors]. (5years fee have to pay at a time)
(iii) Renewal fee as per G.O.Ms.No. 90 Home (PRISONS & FIRE SERVICES) DEPARTMENT Dated 13.08.2021:(5years fee have to pay at a time)
 a) 50% Statutory fee paid on built-up area during NOC, covered under Rule 15 of A.P. Fire Services Act 1999 and AP Fire and Emergency Operations of levy of Fee Rules, 2006.
 b) 25% of the Statutory fee building not covered under Rule (15) of A.P. Fire Service Act, 1999 and A.P. Fire & Emergency Operations and Levy of Fee Rules, 2006.

Whether the Bank Guarantee is required?

No Bank Guarantee is required.

Can any one apply for occupancy NOC without Provisional NOC?

Yes.

What are the Safety Measures suggested for reasonably mitigating the electrical short-circuits in hospital buildings or other high-rise buildings?

i) Installation of Miniature Circuit Breakers, MCBs
ii) No Overloading or every bed to have a power socket in ICU’s.
iii) 10 years old wiring to be changed specially wherever A/c is there
iv) LED lights in Closed Rooms, Corridors, and Staircases connected to inverter (Battery).
v) Grounding/earthing shall be done fool proof.
vi) Electrical Supervisor shall certify all the above safety measures.
vii) For above 500 bed capacity buildings non pressurised Aerosol suppression system / CO2 flooding system in Electrical Panel Rooms
viii) Lightening conductors may be provided for high rise buildings

How many staircases are required for the hospital building?

At least two staircases are required and those are preferably located remote from each other.

How Provisional NOCs will be issued?

The Provisional NOCs will be issued based on the verification of documents and plan drawings submitted by the applicant without physical inspection of the sites by the departmental officers.

How to submit the Plans?

Plans are NOT required to be submitted, if the applicant uploads the building details like height of the building, floor wise built-up areas, type of occupancy and staircase details in online format.

Who can operate the Fire Fighting equipment?

Specifically the staff, Management and other auxiliary staff in the Hospital can operate the plunger based fire equipment very easily at any emergency. Further, Due to simple in operation, anyone can operate the plunger pumps with limited expertise and they can handle and douse the fires.

What is the procedure for making changes like Name and Address in the NOCs already obtained?

Submit the physical letter stating the changes to be made at O/o the Director General of Fire Services or in the online Portal whichever is convenient to the Management.

Any other charges like Penal Charges, User Charges..etc are required for obtaining any NOC?

Except Fire Precaution Fee, no other additional fees is to be paid to the Government or to the department.

What is the periodicity given to occupancy and Renewal NOCs?

As per G.O.Ms.No.90, Home (Prisons & Fire) Department, Dt: 13.08.2021 and G.O.Ms.No.120, Home (Prisons & Fire) Department, Dt: 25.10.2021, Five years periodicity is given for occupancy and Renewal NOCs as well.

If the applicant has conditional NOC or status certificate what is to be done?

If the applicant has conditional NOC or Status certificate issued by this department a fresh occupancy certificate has to be obtained by the owner/occupier by fulfilling the new fire safety measures as prescribed.

If the building has no setbacks, can the owner/occupier apply for fire NOC?

Yes, the owner/occupier can apply for fire NOC, if the building plan meets local/municipal law requirements. Setbacks & Open spaces are subject of local authorities. Fire Services Department is concerned with “Fire Safety” equipment installation only.

If the building has no second staircase, can the owner/occupier can apply for Fire NOC?

Second staircase is mandatory for all buildings which require fire NOC. However, if there is no space to provide the 2nd staircase, the following alternative exits can be provided.
(a) Connecting to neighbourhood building.
(b) Slider
(c) Tube lift with exhaust fn or German chute

Is lift can be considered as alternative to staircase?

No lift can not be considered as second staircase. Because in case of fire, lift can not be used and it requires electricity for operation and also it can lead to smoke logging. However, Tube lift with exhaust fan provided external side of the building can be considered as alternative to second staircase.

Is it necessary to install the Fire Equipment by Fire consultants?

No. the present system of installing 2HP/5HP pumps need not require any Fire consultants. The normal plumber or electrician can install.

Where these 2HP/5HP plunger pumps are available?

These pumps are available with ISI mark in the market from 0.5 HP capacity to 200 HP. Indian companies like Greentech, Kisan, Ultrajet, Pressure jet and Ambica tools are the leading manufacturing of these plunger pumps.
